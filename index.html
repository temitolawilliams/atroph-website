We are a Solar-inverter alternate energy company, located in Lagos

Atroph Energy was founded in 2019 and has tremendously helped individuals and organisations in transforming their energy consumption, provided an eco-friendly alternate source of energy and reduction in energy consumption bills.

We do this by first observing individual or organisation energy need, we then use our experience and expertise to determine the best solar-inverter or inverter systems that will help to bring a better energy utiliy experience so as to increase productivity.

We have installed solar-inverter systems both far and near, in cities like Lagos, Enugu, Kaduna. We work on a 70 - 30 basis; you pay 70% of the total money for the work to be completed so that we can get all the necessary equipments and move the the gadgets to your location and upon completion you pay up the 30% balance

Inverter Systems

With a wide range between 1 - 100KVA, we can cater for both individual and corporate power needs These inverter systems effectively store power in Deep cycle or Lithium Batteries, and then effectively, discharges the power when there is a cut off of power supply either automatically or manually as required in the design. When properly used, these system can serve for long. All products have 12 Months Warranty
